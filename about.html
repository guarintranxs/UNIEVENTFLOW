ABOUT

UniEventFlow is a web-based project management system that provides a centralized platform for student organizations to streamline the planning, submission, and approval workflow of their event Projects through features like status tracking, collaboration, and role-based access. The system aims to improve coordination, reduce manual efforts, and enhance the success of student organization activities at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines Santa Maria, Bulacan Campus. UniEventFlow automates and optimizes critical workflows to help student organizations execute events and initiatives more efficiently.
